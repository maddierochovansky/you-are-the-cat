HOW TO PLAY
YOU ARE A CAT. THE HUMAN JUST LEFT. THIS IS YOUR HOUSE NOW.
THE BASICS
You play through 4 chapters — Kitchen, Living Room, Bedroom, and The Void — making 7 decisions each. Each decision presents 3 choices. Choose one. The human comes home at the end. What they find determines your ending.
CHAOS METER
Chaos tracks how destroyed the house is. Choices marked +CHAOS raise it. It does not go down on its own — only GOOD CAT choices lower it. High chaos unlocks the most unhinged endings. Chaos hitting 100 does nothing on its own — keep going.
SUSPICION METER
Suspicion tracks whether the human will figure out what you did. Warning: if suspicion hits 100, the game ends immediately — the neighbor called, the human came home early, you are caught mid-operation. Keep it below 100 to finish the run. GOOD CAT choices reduce both meters.
CHOICE TYPES
Each choice shows what it affects and how much.

CHAOS + — mild chaos raise. CHAOS ++ — moderate. CHAOS +++ — significant.
SUSP + / SUSP ++ / SUSP +++ — suspicion raises. Watch this one — 100 ends the run.
GOOD CAT - — mild calm. GOOD CAT -- — solid. GOOD CAT --- — significant. All lower both meters. Enough of these unlocks a secret ending.
NEUTRAL — no meter change. Sometimes these are the strangest choices.
INTERRUPTIONS
Random events can fire between decisions. These are things that just happen — a lit cigarette, a spider, a second refrigerator. Handle them. They also affect your meters. Some only trigger under specific conditions. Some unlock cats. Some are very strange.
UNLOCKABLE CATS
Each cat plays with different flavor text and personality. New cats unlock by meeting specific conditions across your runs — finishing a run, specific endings, specific choices, hidden events. The Secrets screen has hints. Some cats have no hints. The SECRETS & UNLOCKS screen tracks your progress.
EACH RUN IS DIFFERENT
Choices are drawn randomly from larger pools each run, so you will not see the same three options twice. Narrations vary. Random events are one-shot per run — once seen, they will not repeat. There are 15+ endings, 4 secret endings, and at least one ending that requires leaving the planet. The decisions themselves also change run to run — you will not see the same scenarios twice.

SECRET ENDINGS
THE AGREEMENT
Interrogate the other cat, then follow them, then go back for them at the door.

FULL VOID
Reach chaos 85+ and choose not to go home at the very end.

THE LONG CON
Drag the treats, release the bookshelf thing, and release the closet thing. All three.

WITNESSED
Make at least 8 calm choices across the whole run, finishing with suspicion under 15.

THE THREAD
Follow the string. Then follow where that leads.
VOID CAT UNLOCK
Find all 4 secret endings across any number of runs. Each ending is tracked. Once all 4 are found, the Void Cat unlocks permanently.
HIDDEN CATS
SCOTTISH FOLD — Trigger the ghost easter egg in Chapter 3.
MR. BISCUITS — Use food-related choices 3+ times in one run. Watch the windows.
CREAM — Finish a run with chaos under 20.
RUSSIAN BLUE — Complete runs with 3 different cats.
THE GHOST — Survive the ghost easter egg and reach the end of the run.
CAPTAIN ONE-EYE — Reach max chaos with suspicion over 60. Claim everything.
